(rem-text "A warning 2 days before Christmas" :d 25 :m Dec :warning-days 2)

Reminders can be made for week days too

(rem-text "Put the bin out" :day-of-week Monday)

Warnings, repeats and day of week parameters can be combined into reminders. The rules for combining parameters aim to be similar to remind. If the day of week doesn’t match the date entered, the reminder will trigger on the next occurence of the weekday. The following reminder will trigger on the 25th of July.

(rem-text "Occurs on Saturday 25th July" :day-of-week Sat :m 7 :d 24)

To send all reminders for today, use send-reminder-emails-digest

(send-reminder-emails-digest "me@myemail.com")
