Applications, modules and submodules

To group modules into an application use application.

application: CMS

Each module has submodules grouped using module.

module: Article

Example: (E-Commerce Application)

Product:
  application: ECommerce
Client:
  application: ECommerce
Category:
  module: Product
OptionType:
  module: Product

Example: (Blog)

Post:
  application: Blog
Category:
  application: Blog
Tag:
  module: Post
